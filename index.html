THE PROBLEM

North East India's
Water Challenges

Standard purifiers fail here. Our systems are engineered for these exact conditions.

🌧️

High Monsoon Turbidity

Seasonal flooding dramatically increases suspended solids, mud, silt and clay in water sources.

⚙️

Iron & Manganese Contamination

Underground and surface sources carry excess iron and manganese that standard filters can't handle.

🦠

Microbial Load

Surface water sources carry high levels of bacteria and pathogens, particularly during floods.

📊

Variable TDS & Hardness

TDS and water hardness fluctuate significantly across seasons and geography in the region.

💨

Unstable Water Pressure

Inconsistent pressure and seasonal supply changes make conventional systems unreliable.

🛠️

Our Answer

Custom multi-stage pre-filtration + RO systems engineered specifically for these challenges.
